About the Ensemble

The Civic Symphony Concert Band is a 60-member volunteer ensemble comprised of musicians from all walks of life. From music teachers to engineers, we are united by our passion for wind band literature.

Under the direction of Maestro John Doe, we perform four major concerts a year, dedicated to preserving the rich history of the concert band tradition while exploring modern compositions.
